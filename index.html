My Approach

Colorful, editorial, authentically told

You asked for vibrancy, candid moments, fashion-forward detail shots, and black and white throughout. My approach is always collaborative. I want to know what you love, what you don't, what matters most to you, so that what we create together is uniquely and unmistakably you. Here is how I work.

01
Color and Energy

You'll see from my portfolio that my work loves colour. Whether it's the rich golds of a haldi or the electric glow of a midnight reception, I believe in representing real-life colour boldly and with a luxury aesthetic. No matter where in the world I'm shooting, colour is never an afterthought.

02
Editorial Eye

Fashion-forward detail shots are a strength of mine. Your outfits, the jewelry, the ceremony space at The Arlo. I'll find the frame others miss. I'll also guide and direct you in a seamless way to ensure we get those editorial shots, still in a relaxed, effortless way that never feels forced or awkward.

03
Candid and Documentary

My approach at heart is documentary. I photograph what is actually happening, not what is posed. 200 guests across four events means an extraordinary abundance of real, unrepeatable moments. The tears, the laughter, the chaos and the calm. I catch all of it.

04
Black and White

Some moments are best told without colour. I weave black and white throughout your gallery as a counterpoint. Intimate, timeless, emotionally direct. It gives the gallery a depth and range that colour alone cannot achieve.

Coverage Plan

Your weekend, fully covered

Based on your brief, 18 hours of coverage across Saturday and Sunday, four distinct events, and around 200 guests. Here is exactly how I'll approach each one.

Saturday
April 17, 2027
Haldi and Mehendi, Morning

This is where the colour begins. The turmeric, the laughter, the patterns being drawn by hand. I'll document every detail and every candid moment. The kind of images that tell the story of your culture and your families coming together.

Welcome Party, Downtown Austin Evening

Downtown Austin at night has incredible energy and light. I'll capture the arrival, the fashion, the first moments of your guests celebrating together. Editorial and documentary in approach, alive in feeling.

Sunday
April 18, 2027
Ceremony at The Arlo, Morning

An early morning ceremony means extraordinary light. I'll be there ahead of you, scouting the space and ready the moment you arrive. The Arlo is known for its beautiful natural light, and that is one of the great strengths you'll see woven throughout my work. Every reaction, every tear, the moment you become husband and wife.

Cocktail Hour and Reception, 6 PM to Midnight

Six hours of celebration. The toasts, the first dance, the full energy of 200 guests until midnight. I stay present and focused through every moment. This is where the real story lives.
